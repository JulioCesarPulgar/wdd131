Site Plan

Site Name

Digital Library of Open Access Books

This name clearly communicates the site's purpose: a curated, easily accessible collection of high-quality, free-to-read books. It emphasizes the open-access and educational mission, making it discoverable and trustworthy for users seeking public domain or creative commons literature.

Optional domain availability: openaccesslibrary.org

Site Purpose

The site serves as a portal for discovering and reading open-access books. It provides a searchable, filterable catalog of fiction and non-fiction works, with no registration or payment required. The goal is to promote universal access to knowledge and support the open-access movement.

Scenarios

  • How can I find free, high-quality books on a specific topic or by a particular author?
  • What is open-access literature, and where do these books come from?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #2a4d69 — Used for headings, logo, and navigation highlights.
  • Accent Color: #f4b942 — Used for buttons, links, and call-to-action elements.
  • Background: #f9f9f9 — Used for page background.

Typography

  • Merriweather (serif) — Used for headings and logo for a scholarly, trustworthy feel.
  • Open Sans (sans-serif) — Used for body text for readability and modern appearance.
