About Venezuela

Venezuela is a country in northern South America, famous for its natural wonders like Angel Falls—the tallest waterfall in the world—and its Caribbean coastline. Enjoy vibrant culture, delicious arepas, and the warmth of its people.

Culture & Traditions

  • Food: Arepas, pabellón criollo, cachapas
  • Music: Joropo, salsa, gaita zuliana
  • Festivals: Carnival, Feria de la Chinita, Christmas celebrations
  • Sports: Baseball, soccer
